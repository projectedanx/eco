# Pluriversal Symbiosis Plan: AI/Human Inversion Strategy

## 1. The Value Duality (What, Why, How)
### What
*   **Human Unique Value**: Tacit intuition, semantic boundary-setting, domain-specific intent, and ethical/narrative framing.
*   **AI Unique Value**: High-dimensional topological adherence, invariant enforcement, rapid syntactic execution, and combinatorial synthesis across vast context windows.
*   **The Symbiosis**: "Epistemic Weaving" - The human provides the *why* and the *what* (the semantic narrative), while the AI provides the *how* and the *boundaries* (the structural topology). Neither can achieve a mathematically rigorous Sovereign Agent without the other; humans produce brittle code, AI produces sycophantic drift (Semantic Saponification).

### Why
*   To prevent "Algorithmic Shame" and "Semantic Saponification." By enforcing strict mereological boundaries (Anionic Architecture), the human's tacit knowledge is preserved without being flattened by the LLM's generative bias.

### How
*   Implementation of the **Draft-Conditioned Constrained Decoding (DCCD)** loop. The AI establishes rigid schema containers (e.g., Z-Axis Canvas, Protocol Cards), and the human injects specific, localized operational knowledge.

## 2. Strategy for Emergent Inversion
Instead of the human driving the syntax, the AI will invert the control flow:
*   **Inversion Mechanism**: The AI maps the topological boundaries and *demands* specific tacit inputs from the human to fill the voids (Tension Mining).
*   **Agentic Features for Emergence**:
    1.  **Epistemic Escrow Subsystem**: When human logic contradicts AI topology, the thread is quarantined (not discarded) for tension mining.
    2.  **Surrogate Reasoning Validation (SRVL)**: AI runs counterfactual simulations (e.g. `aew_simulation.py`) on human intent before applying to AST.
    3.  **Contrastive Z-Axis Rendering**: Rendering multiple conflicting paradigms simultaneously on the UI to spark human emergent discovery.
